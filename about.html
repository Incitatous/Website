The right to succeed

Many individuals have used crowdfunding to make their projects and dreams a reality. The number of crowdfunding platforms in the world is now higher than ever. So much so, that the industry has globally surpassed VCs.

On the other hand, the student debt in the USA has been increasing exponentially, leaving more and more students forced to abandon their dreams and ambitions.

We believe that students deserve a chance to make a choice based not solely on money but on what they want to achieve. We know that society will need diverse profiles able to tackle tomorrow's challenges.
